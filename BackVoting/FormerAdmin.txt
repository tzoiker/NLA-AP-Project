<p>This page documents <b>former Wikipedia <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Administrators" title="Wikipedia:Administrators">administrators</a></b>. There are four lists: alphabetical, chronological, by reason of desysop and by number of administrative actions. There are <b><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Former_administrators/full" title="Wikipedia:Former administrators/full">736</a></b> former administrator accounts, including alternate accounts used by the same individual (last updated October 2, 2014). For a plain full list of former administrators, see <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Former_administrators/full" title="Wikipedia:Former administrators/full">Wikipedia:Former administrators/full</a>.</p>
